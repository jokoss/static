About Motzz Laboratory

Established in 2004, Motzz Laboratory is a minority and woman-owned testing laboratory providing comprehensive testing services across multiple industries. Our ISO 17025 certified facility serves clients throughout Arizona, Nevada, and California.

We specialize in agricultural nutrient testing, environmental analysis, construction materials testing, dietary supplement analysis, and microbiology services. Our commitment to accuracy and quality is demonstrated through participation in multiple proficiency testing programs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our testing services

Turnaround & Timing

How long does it take to get test results?

Turnaround times vary by test type. Most tests are completed within 3-7 business days. Rush services are available for urgent needs with 1-3 day turnaround. Contact us for specific timing on your required tests.

Do you offer rush services?

Yes, we offer rush services for urgent testing needs. Rush services typically provide results within 1-3 business days, depending on the test type. Additional fees may apply for rush services.

Certifications & Quality

What certifications does Motzz Laboratory have?

We are ISO/IEC 17025: 2017 certified and licensed by the Arizona Department of Health Services, Arizona Department of Agriculture, and Arizona Department of Transportation. We participate in multiple proficiency testing programs to ensure accuracy.

How do you ensure test accuracy?

We maintain strict quality control measures, participate in proficiency testing programs, and follow standardized testing protocols. Our ISO 17025 certification ensures our methods meet international standards for testing accuracy.

Sample Collection & Shipping

Do you provide sample collection services?

Yes, we can provide sample collection services for many types of tests. We also offer consultation on proper sample collection techniques to ensure accurate results. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

How should I prepare and ship samples?

Sample preparation varies by test type. We provide detailed collection instructions for each test. Samples should be properly labeled, packaged, and shipped according to our guidelines to maintain sample integrity.

Service Areas & Coverage

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Arizona, Nevada, and California. Our laboratory is located in Phoenix, AZ, and we can accommodate shipping samples from other locations. Contact us to discuss your specific location and needs.

Do you serve residential and commercial clients?

Yes, we serve both residential and commercial clients. Our services are available to homeowners, farmers, construction companies, environmental consultants, and various other industries requiring laboratory testing.
